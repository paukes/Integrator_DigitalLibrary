# Hydrology - Resources

## Surface Water

### Alberta River Basins

-   Link: <https://rivers.alberta.ca/>
    -   Organization: Provincial
    -   What it has:
        -   Long-term data: water level; discharge; precipitation; snow stations

            -   ***Note:** You are limited to one-year of observations (need to email them for longer records)*

### Water Survey of Canada

-   Link: <https://wateroffice.ec.gc.ca/>

    -   Organization: Federal

    -   What it has:

        -   Long term data: water level; discharge
        -   Real-time hydrometric data

## Groundwater

### GOWN - Alberta Groundwater Observation Well Network

-   Link: <https://www.alberta.ca/lookup/groundwater-observation-well-network.aspx>

    -   Organization: Provincial

    -   What it has:

        -   Long-term data: groundwater level

### Alberta Water Wells

-   Link: <https://groundwater.alberta.ca/WaterWells/d/>

    -   Organization: Provincial

    -   What it has:

        -   Drilling details for \~all wells drilled in Alberta (after \~1970)

### Rocky View Well Watch

-   Link: <https://gwfnet.net/Metadata/Record/T-2021-11-30-819ems3z82AkWwZ81isEQt4nw>

    -   Organization: Academia

    -   What is has:

        -   Long-term data: groundwater levels
            -   Rural wells around Rocky View County area
